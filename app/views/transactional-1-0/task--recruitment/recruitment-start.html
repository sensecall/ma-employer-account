Advertise an apprenticeship

Recruit an apprentice by creating a vacancy. Vacancies will be advertised on Find an apprenticeship.

What you will need

Before you create your vacancy, you’ll need to choose appropriate apprenticeship training and get some information from your training provider. If you don’t have a training provider yet, you can find a provider.

You’ll need the following:

  • the name of the apprenticeship training and training provider
  • the duration of the apprenticeship
  • the amount of hours spent working and training
  • the amount you’d like to pay your apprentice

It should take no longer than 30 minutes to create your first vacancy
