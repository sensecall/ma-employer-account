Reserve funding to train and assess an apprentice

Apprenticeship funding can pay for part or all of the cost of training and assessing an apprentice (up to the funding band maximum). You may need to pay up to 5% of the total cost.

You need to reserve funding to guarantee that we'll be able to pay your training provider for apprenticeship training and assessment.

You'll need to know:

  • approximately when you expect the apprenticeship training to start
  • the apprenticeship training course the apprentice will take
