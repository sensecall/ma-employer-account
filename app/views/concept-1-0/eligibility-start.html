Set up an apprenticeship for an existing employee

What you'll need to do:

  1. Reserve funding for apprenticeship training and assessment (if you have not already).
  2. Add the employee's details.

What you'll need

You’ll need to know:

  • the name of the apprenticeship training course
  • which month the employee will start their apprenticeship training
  • your training provider’s UK Provider Reference Number (UKPRN)
  • the employee’s name and date of birth
  • the cost of the apprenticeship training (as agreed with your training provider)

Set up an apprenticeship for a new employee

What you'll need to do:

  1. Reserve funding for apprenticeship training and assessment.
  2. Create an advert (if you want to advertise your apprenticeship).
  3. Decide which apprentice you want to take on.
  4. Sign in to your account and add the apprentice's details.

You do not have to set up an apprenticeship in one go. You can complete each section when you're ready.
