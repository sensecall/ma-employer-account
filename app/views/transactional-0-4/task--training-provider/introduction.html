Training provider

What is a training provider?

A training provider is the organisation that will deliver the apprentice's training course, sometimes called "off-the-job training".

The training provider could be:

  • a college
  • a university
  • an academy
  • a large national training company
  • your organisation

Finding a training provider

To search for training providers and apprenticeship training courses, you can use the Find apprenticeship training (opens in a new tab) service.

Becoming an apprenticeship training provider

Some organisations may train their own employees rather than using a third party.
