What happens next

We’ll review your advert within one working day.

If we need you to make any changes, the advert will show in the ‘Rejected adverts’ section of your dashboard. We’ll also send you an email to let you know (only if you’ve set up email notifications).

Once we’ve approved your advert:

  • it will show in your ‘Live adverts’
  • we’ll post it on Find an apprenticeship (opens in a new tab or window)

Hiring an apprentice

We’ll send you email notifications to let you know when you get new applications.

You’ll need to:

  • view the applications and decide if you want to interview any of the candidates
  • interview candidates, for example over the phone and face-to-face
  • contact the candidates to let them know the outcome of their interviews
  • add any successful candidates’ details to the ‘Apprentices’ section of your account
