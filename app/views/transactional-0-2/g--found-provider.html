Training provider

What is a training provider?

A training provider is the organisation that will deliver the apprentice's training course.

Training providers might referred to by other names:

  • college
  • university
  • academy

Your organisation may choose to deliver the apprenticeship training and assessment rather than using a third party.

Finding a training provider

To search for training providers and apprenticeship training courses, you can use the Find apprenticeship training (opens in new tab) service.
